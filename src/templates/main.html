Wells

Traditionally a Tory stronghold, the seat was seized by Tessa Munt for the Lib Dems in 2010 but regained by Conservative James Heappey two years ago. A strong local campaign by the Lib Dems will be needed to wrest it back. The seat includes the cathedral city, the seaside town of Burnham-on-Sea, Glastonbury, Cheddar and Shepton Mallet. Agriculture and tourism are the most important industries.
