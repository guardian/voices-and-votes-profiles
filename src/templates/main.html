Constituency profile

Glasgow East

Glasgow East, like the rest of the city, switched from Labour to Scottish National Party in 2015. The constituency's MP Natalie McGarry – then SNP, now independent – saw off Labour veteran Margaret Curran with a swing of 30%.

70,378 population

10,387 majority

56% voted remain

30% of children ranked as in poverty

£460 median weekly wage (compared to £530 average figure)

20.4% of 16-64 year olds on out-of-work benefits (2016)
