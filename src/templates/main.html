Erdington

Labour has held this seat since its inception in 1974; it is currently represented by Jack Dromey, who was first elected in 2010. In 2015, his majority was a relatively healthy 5129 – but that makes it a seat the Conservatives could hope to win. Erdington has a rich industrial past, but has struggled to recover from the demise of Fort Dunlop – a booming tyre factory.
