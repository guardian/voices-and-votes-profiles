Harrow West

Harrow West had been Tory since its creation in 1945, but Gareth Thomas won it during Labour’s landslide victory in 1997 and he has held it ever since, albeit with a shrunken majority. Local issues, such as housing and education, are likely to collide with the big one: Brexit.
